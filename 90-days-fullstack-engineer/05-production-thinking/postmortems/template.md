# Postmortem Template

## Incident summary

- **What happened**
- **When it happened**
- **Scope and impact**

## Timeline

- list events with timestamps

## Root cause

- explain the underlying issue

## Impact

- describe affected systems and users

## Mitigation and resolution

- steps taken to restore service

## Prevention

- long-term actions to avoid recurrence

## Lessons learned

- operational improvements
- documentation needs
- process or tooling gaps
