# Senior Portfolio Goals

This repository is designed to demonstrate senior engineering outcomes through:

- **System ownership**: a structured multi-service platform that shows end-to-end thinking.
- **Tradeoff documentation**: explicit design decisions with alternatives and risks.
- **Production resilience**: retry, fallback, monitoring, incident handling, and postmortem discipline.
- **Architecture decisions**: clear service responsibilities, data flow, boundaries, and scaling strategies.
- **Mentoring artifacts**: patterns, guides, templates, and structured thinking for team-level delivery.
- **Delivery under constraints**: environment-driven deployments, secure defaults, and incremental proof points.

A senior signal in this repo means the portfolio is not just a log of daily work; it is a coherent engineering system with measurable progress.
